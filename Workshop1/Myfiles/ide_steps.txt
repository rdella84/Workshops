Steps to create a simple console application in the IDE:

1. Click File->New->Project.

2. Click on Visual C++ and on the screen on the right, click Win32 Console Application

3. Then at the bottom you can name your project and see or change the location where your project is saved. Click OK.

4. A window will pop up and click next. Make sure in the 'Application type' console application is selected.

5. Uncheck 'Precompiled header' and Security Development Lifecycle(SDL)check. Then check 'Empty project'. Click Finish.

6. Now that your console application is created. Right click on source files on 'Solution Explorer' and you have the option to add new items or existing items.
